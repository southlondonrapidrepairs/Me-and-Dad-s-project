FAQs

Clear answers

Money handling: We do not hold client funds. Funds are held and released by our escrow/PBA partner.

If you still have questions after reading this, message us and we’ll answer in plain English.

Money & escrow/PBA
Do you hold client money?+
No. We do not hold client funds. Funds are held and released by our escrow/PBA partner.
Where do the funds sit?+
Funds sit in an escrow / Project Bank Account arrangement operated by the partner for the project.
Who gets paid?+
Payees (suppliers/contractors) get paid directly when the milestone is approved and a release is triggered.
When are payments released?+
Only after evidence is provided and the milestone is approved. If there’s an issue, the release can be paused.
Process
What is a milestone?+
A milestone is a clear stage of work, with a clear amount and clear “done” checks.
What counts as evidence?+
Photos, delivery notes, signed checklists, PDFs, links to deliverables, or a short site note.
Can approvals be shared?+
Yes. You can set roles: client-only approval, or client + manager approval.
Can a payee request payment early?+
They can request, but it won’t release unless the milestone rules are met and approved.
VAT
Do you guarantee VAT savings?+
No. VAT depends on the project and each supplier’s VAT status. Savings vary. We charge VAT on our management fee where applicable. We don’t provide tax advice.
Why can VAT differ?+
Because suppliers have different VAT status and different projects can have different VAT rules. Always confirm with your accountant.
Do trade invoices include VAT?+
Sometimes. It depends on whether that supplier/contractor is VAT registered and on the type of work.
Disputes & pauses
What if there is a dispute?+
You can pause a release, record the reason, and follow steps to resolve it. Everything stays logged.
What if work is partly done?+
You can split milestones or set partial approvals if your project rules allow. Keep it clear and documented.
Can we add retention/holdbacks?+
Yes. You can set a final milestone for snagging or retention release, with clear checks.
Security
How do you prevent bank detail fraud?+
Bank details are verified, changes are flagged, and approvals are required. Always confirm changes using a trusted method.
Do you store documents?+
Yes, project evidence and logs are stored so you can prove what happened. See our Privacy page for details.
Construction (South London service)
Are you the main contractor?+
No. In our Construction Management setup, we act as Construction Manager / Agent. The client may contract with trades/suppliers where applicable.
What do you manage weekly?+
Programme, trade coordination, milestone checks, variation control, weekly updates, and snag tracking.
Do you manage variations?+
Yes. Variations should be priced and approved before work starts, with timeline impact recorded.
