How it’s structured

Clear roles

  • We are not the main contractor.
  • We act as Construction Manager / Agent.

Payments

Secure staged releases

  • Milestones are agreed up front.
  • Trades submit invoices + evidence.
  • You approve milestones.
  • Partner releases payment to payees.
  • Full audit trail is kept.
