How it works

Milestones → Evidence → Approval → Release

Money handling: We do not hold client funds. Funds are held and released by our escrow/PBA partner.

This is built for real projects. You only release payment when a milestone is approved. Every action is logged.

Simple money-flow diagram
Client funds
→
Escrow / PBA partner account
→
Release on approved milestone
→
Payee paid directly

The partner holds the funds. Approvals control releases. We provide the workflow, proof, and audit trail.

Step 1

Set up the project

  • Add payees (suppliers/contractors).
  • Create milestones with clear “done” rules.
  • Attach documents (scope, drawings, brief).
  • Choose who can approve (client only, or client + manager).
Step 2

Collect evidence and approve

  • Payees submit invoices against milestones.
  • Upload evidence (photos, documents, sign-off notes).
  • Approve or reject with a reason.
  • Every decision is recorded.
Step 3

Release payments via the partner

  • Once approved, a release is triggered.
  • The escrow/PBA partner pays the payee directly.
  • The payment reference is logged.
  • You can export a full report.
VAT note: VAT depends on the project and each supplier’s VAT status. Savings vary. We charge VAT on our management fee where applicable. We don’t provide tax advice.
Disputes

Clear pause rules

If something isn’t right, you can pause a release and record the reason.

  • Pause and notify the payee.
  • List what needs fixing.
  • Upload proof when resolved.
  • Approve and continue.
Audit trail

Everything is logged

  • Who approved what.
  • What evidence was provided.
  • When funds were released.
  • Exports for accountants and records.
