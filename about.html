About

Built for simple, fair payments

Money handling: We do not hold client funds. Funds are held and released by our escrow/PBA partner.
Our mission

Less confusion. Less risk.

We believe payments should follow proof. Not pressure. If work is done and approved, it should be paid quickly. If it is not done, payment should wait.

What we stand for
  • Simple language and clear steps.
  • Evidence-based approvals.
  • Clean logs and exports.
  • Transparent partner-held funds.
