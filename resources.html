Resources

Easy guides and templates

Money handling: We do not hold client funds. Funds are held and released by our escrow/PBA partner.

Use these to run clean projects: clear milestones, clear approvals, and clean records.

Guide 1

How to write a good milestone

  • Make it measurable (“what does done look like?”).
  • List the evidence required (photos, docs, sign-off).
  • Set a clear amount and a clear due date.
  • Avoid vague milestones like “progress payment”.
Guide 2

How to handle changes (variations)

  • Write the change in one sentence.
  • Confirm the price change in writing.
  • Confirm the time change (days/weeks).
  • Approve before work starts.
Guide 3

How to prevent bank detail fraud

  • Verify payee bank details at onboarding.
  • Flag and re-check any bank change request.
  • Confirm using a trusted phone number.
  • Keep a log of who confirmed it.
Guide 4

How to run a weekly update

  • 3 photos minimum (before/after).
  • What was done this week.
  • What happens next week.
  • Any blockers and decisions needed.
Contact form setup

EmailJS or Formspree

This site uses a simple HTML form. Choose one:

  • Formspree: replace the form action URL with your Formspree endpoint.
  • EmailJS: add the EmailJS script and keys, then send via JavaScript.

Tip: add a hidden “honeypot” field and reject submissions where it is filled.
